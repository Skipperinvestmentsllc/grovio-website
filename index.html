The Problem

Homeschooling is beautiful.
The paperwork isn't.

Between attendance logs, portfolio photos, and figuring out your state's rules — it's easy to feel buried. You started homeschooling for freedom, not spreadsheets.

"I have attendance in three different notebooks and none of them are complete."
"Our best work is scattered across my camera roll with no way to organize it."
"I don't even know what my state actually requires. Every source says something different."
"The apps I've tried are overwhelming — they feel like they were built for schools, not families."
Not a planner. A rhythm.
Grovio doesn't give you a rigid schedule. It gives you a gentle daily rhythm — a flow of activities you can check off, rearrange, or skip. Because real homeschool days aren't linear.
Features

Simple tools for real
homeschool life

Attendance tracking
One-tap daily attendance for each learner. Export anytime as PDF or CSV for your state's requirements.
Daily rhythm
A flexible daily flow — not a rigid schedule. Add activities, check them off, adjust as your day unfolds.
Photo portfolio
Capture and organize your children's work beautifully. Add notes, tag by subject, and build a portfolio you're proud of.
Shareable links
Generate a beautiful read-only link to share your child's portfolio with family, evaluators, or your umbrella school.
The Grovio Guide
Practical articles on getting started, record-keeping, daily rhythm, and common questions — written by a homeschool mom, not a textbook.
Works offline
Use Grovio without an account or internet connection. Your data stays on your device until you choose to sync.
