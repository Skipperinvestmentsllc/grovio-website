The Problem

Homeschooling is beautiful.
The paperwork isn't.

You're not just teaching. You're tracking attendance. Saving work samples. Trying to remember what counted as "school" today. And somehow… keeping it all organized.

Most tools weren't built for this kind of life. They expect structure. You're living something much more fluid.

Most homeschool tools are built like school systems. grovio isn't.

I have attendance in three different notebooks and none of them are complete.

Our best work is scattered across my camera roll with no way to organize it.

The apps I've tried are overwhelming — they feel like they were built for schools, not families.

Not a planner. A rhythm.

grovio works the way your days actually unfold: slow mornings, spontaneous learning, deep dives, and off days. You don't have to force structure just to prove learning happened.

That's grovio.

Works where you homeschool.

grovio is designed for the way most U.S. families homeschool. Whether your state is completely hands-off or asks for an annual notice and assessment, you'll have organized attendance records, a beautiful portfolio, and clean exports ready whenever you need them.
