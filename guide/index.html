Still learning. Everything here comes from real life — mine and the moms around me.

I'm Claire — a homeschool mom of two who learned most of this the hard way. We stepped away from traditional school five years ago, and honestly? The first year was a lot of Googling at midnight and second-guessing everything.

These days I also help run our local homeschool co-op, which means I get to learn alongside a whole community of moms who are figuring this out together. That's made me a much better guide than I ever could have been on my own.

The grovio Guide is everything I wish someone had handed me in year one — no jargon, no judgment, just what actually works for real families.
