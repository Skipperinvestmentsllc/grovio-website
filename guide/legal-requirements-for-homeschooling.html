🌱 Getting Started

What are the legal requirements for homeschooling in my state?

C

Claire

4 min read · The Grovio Guide

I'm not a lawyer and I'm definitely not your state's homeschool officer — so please use this as a starting point and always verify with your state's official requirements.

When we decided to homeschool, the legal question was the first thing I looked up. Probably at midnight.

I braced for something complicated. What I found was much simpler than I thought.

Homeschooling is legal in all 50 states, but the requirements vary. Some states ask for a simple notification. Others require basic record keeping or an annual review.

Most are more manageable than they sound.

The two resources I always recommend are HSLDA's state law chart and your state homeschool association. They break things down in a way that actually makes sense.

Once you understand what your state requires, a lot of the background anxiety disappears.

You don't need to memorize everything.

You just need to know where to look.
