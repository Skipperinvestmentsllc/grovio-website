Common questions

Can I use grovio without an account?
Yes — grovio works fully offline without an account. Create an account to back up your data and access it across devices.

How do I export my attendance records?
Go to the Record tab, tap the export button in the top right, and choose PDF or CSV.

How do I share my child's portfolio?
Go to the Portfolio tab, select a learner, and tap the share icon in the header. A link will be copied to your clipboard.

How do I delete my account?
Email us at hello@grovioapp.com and we will delete your account and all data within 30 days.
